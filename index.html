I am an assistant professor in the mathematics department at Virginia Tech. Previously, I was a post-doctoral fellow in the Institute for Quantum Computing at the University of Waterloo. I received my PhD in 2018 from Penn State University, and my advisor was Kirsten Eisenträger. I am interested in cryptography, explicit methods in number theory, and definability and decidability in number theory. With Leonardo Mihalcea and Rahul Singh, I am coorganizing the algebra seminar at Virginia Tech.
